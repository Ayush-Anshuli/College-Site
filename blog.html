Our Certificate & Online Programs For 2023

The value of receiving an education from a historically Black college and university (HBCU) in Atlanta has a unique advantage. Not only does the city's vibrant Black community provide a rich cultural environment, giving students a sense of belonging and empowerment, but Atlanta also serves as a hub for HBCU employers, offering ample internship, job, and networking opportunities.


Atlanta is home to four of the nation's top HBCUs, all less than a mile apart from each other. The Morehouse School of Medicine excels in healthcare education. Spelman College and Morehouse College both offer comprehensive liberal arts programs, while Clark Atlanta University is a renowned research institution that provides many disciplines to choose from.


One interesting HBCU fact is that all are members of The Atlanta University Center Consortium, the world's oldest and largest association of HBCUs. Among the member benefits is cross registration, meaning students can enroll in courses at any of these institutions. There are also shared community partnerships, resources, and engagement.


At BestColleges, we believe a college education is one of the most important investments you can make. We want to help you navigate the college selection process by offering transparent, inclusive, and relevant school rankings.
